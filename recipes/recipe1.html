Plagued Spirit Bull Rat Liquor

Description:

Ingredients

  • 1 grown bull rat (alive!)
  • 9 red potatoes
  • 3 drops of wormwood oil

Steps

  1. Drain rat's blood into large cooking pot
  2. Boil with potatoes on red hot stove
  3. Add wormword when a mash forms
  4. Strain the brew into copper vats with the rat corpse
  5. Swelter one full moon cycle
  6. Strain into a bottle WEAR A MASK OR YOU'LL PUKE!
  7. Seal the rat inside with the liquor
